Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams, with the primary objective of scoring runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets. Matches can span several days (Test cricket), a day (One Day Internationals), or a few hours (Twenty20), offering different formats to suit varying preferences. Bowlers aim to dismiss batsmen, while batsmen strive to accumulate runs, with intricate strategies and diverse playing styles shaping the game. Cricket has a global appeal, particularly popular in countries like India, Australia, England, and South Africa, with major international tournaments such as the Cricket World Cup. The sport is characterized by its gentlemanly traditions, strategic nuances, and the passionate following it enjoys in many cricket-loving nations.
